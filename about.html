Our Story

Connecting world-class talent with unforgettable events since 2010.

Redefining Talent Management

Founded on the belief that extraordinary talent is the cornerstone of any successful event, Kalakaar Studios started as a small boutique agency in Mumbai. Over the past decade, we have grown into India's leading talent booking platform.

We represent a curated roster of artists spanning across music, comedy, motivational speaking, and celebrity endorsements. Our mission is to provide an organized, transparent, and seamless booking experience for event planners, agencies, and brands.
