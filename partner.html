Common Questions

Do you charge a booking fee?

Our pricing is fully transparent. The quoted price includes the artist fee and our agency commission. Travel and technical riders are billed at actuals.

What happens if the artist cancels?

In the rare event of a cancellation due to force majeure, our contracts ensure a 100% refund or an immediate replacement of equal caliber, depending on your preference.

Can you handle international bookings?

Yes. We have a dedicated division for securing international visas, work permits, and global travel logistics for artists flying into India or vice versa.
